Video to canvas with text overlay

Press Play/Pause to start the demo.

A <canvas> element is displayed on the central part of the screen. A <video> element is dynamically created in the DOM to play a sample video. The <video> is not rendered to the screen.

On each repaint, the current frame from the video is painted to the canvas and the current timestamp is drawn over the top of the video frame.

All the key DOM elements are in in global scope, so you can inspect them from the browser console.

  • video - the offscreen video element.
  • videoCanvas - the canvas in the main part of the screen, to which the video frames and timestamp text are painted.
  • canvasCtx - the canvas context used for drawing.
  • playPause - the Play/Pause button DOM element.
